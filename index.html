Our Approach

From discovery to lasting impact

We unite movement leaders, technologists, and strategic partners to build lasting solutions for progressive campaigns and organizations.

1

Discovery

Understanding movement needs and spotting emerging opportunities. We listen to organizers, researchers, and technologists to find where the biggest gaps and possibilities are.

2

Transformation

Developing and testing breakthrough solutions. Through fellowships, hackathons, and research, we build and validate new tools and approaches before they scale.

3

Scale & Sustain

Creating clear pathways for widespread adoption. We publish guides, run trainings, and build communities of practice so innovations reach the organizations that need them.

2025 Media Innovation Fellows

Six projects reimagining progressive media

Selected from over 100 proposals, our inaugural fellows are building tools and playbooks at the intersection of media, technology, and organizing.

Laura Liibbe

WhatsApp Channels Playbook

A playbook for using WhatsApp Channels as high-trust infrastructure to engage Latino and immigrant audiences where they already congregate.

Andrew Markoff

M3 Media Planning Platform

Cross-channel media optimization helping campaigns allocate budgets more effectively for voter persuasion and turnout.

Steven McAlpine

INTRVL Creator Audience Profiling

Political and behavioral insights into creator audiences, helping advocacy campaigns measure and target their creator partnerships.

Jesse Thomas

Project Shoutout

Testing sponsorships with livestream creators on YouTube, Twitch, and Kick to drive measurable civic engagement actions.

Liz Utrup

Real People Project

Online ethnography research uncovering how real voters engage with digital content, informing smarter outreach strategies.

Gabriella Zutrau

Instagram Chatbot Playbook

Recommendations for social media chatbot usage in politics and advocacy, turning online enthusiasm into real-world action.

Each fellow receives a $15,000 grant, mentorship, coaching, and showcase opportunities.
